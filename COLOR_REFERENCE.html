Design Principles

  • Clean & Professional - No flashy gradients or unnecessary effects
  • Elegant Typography - Lora serif font adds sophistication
  • Teal Accent - Strategic use of #1B9C85 for important interactive elements
  • Sharp Corners - border-radius: 0px for modern, crisp aesthetic
  • Subtle Depth - Light shadows instead of heavy effects
  • High Contrast - Dark navy (#0F172A) on white backgrounds for readability
